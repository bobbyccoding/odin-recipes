Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is about to be fire. Do you have friends? You will if you make this. This lasagna is so good your dad will finally tell you he loves you (not a guarantee).

This lasagna is easy to make and very cheap. It requires very little prep work. It is vegan, dairy-free, and gluten-free (not a guarantee)

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Crack
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Mix the sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Smoke some crack.
  4. Make the ricotta mixture
  5. Layer the lasagna
  6. Smoke some more crack
  7. Cover and cook.
  8. Let sit and cool.
